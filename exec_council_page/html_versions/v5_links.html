Executive Council

What is the Executive Council?

The Executive Council (EC) is the senior stakeholder advisory committee to the Board of the IoTAA. The EC comprises a broad mix of corporates, government departments and regulators, industry players, research and industry bodies.

How to Apply

Membership of the EC is by nomination and carries an annual fee. Organisations interested in joining the Executive Council are invited to nominate themselves by writing to the Chairman of the EC setting out their interests in joining. The Executive Council are required to endorse proposed nominees. Upon successful endorsement the Chairman confirms the new member.
