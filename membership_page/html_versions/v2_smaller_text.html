Membership FAQ

What is the purpose of the association?

IoTAA is an industry association dedicated to advancing the adoption and implementation of the Internet of Things (IoT) technologies in Australia.

What are the benefits of becoming a member?

There are many benefits of becoming a member. This will depend on the type of membership as well. For benefits of a workstream member (individual) please click here. And for benefits of a organisation or Executive Council membership please click here.

How much is the membership fee, and what does it cover?

There are 4 types of memberships. They are Workstream Member - $295/annum, Workstream Member (Student) - $20/annum, Workstream Member (Startup) - $150/annum, and Executive Council Membership (organisation membership). This membership is by application and depends on the size of the corporation.

Is membership open to individuals or only to companies/organizations?

Membership is open to individuals or organisations.

How do I apply for membership?

If you are an individual, you can apply for membership by going to https://iot.org.au/join/. Alternatively, if you are representing an organisation, please apply for membership by contacting us.

What is the process for approval of membership applications?

Workstream memberships are approved upon payment of the relevant fee and verification of email address (for contact purposes). Executive Council memberships are by application. There is an internal process that each application has to go through.

Can I attend events or conferences organized by the association as a non-member?

Yes, you can attend events or conferences organized by the association as a non-member. However, members receive special concessions and discounts for events.

Will I receive any publications or newsletters as a member?

Yes, members receive our monthly membership magazine. They also receive other newsletters. Moreover, members will have access to a range of documents and artifacts developed by the association.

How can I access networking opportunities with other members?

This is achieved by attending conferences and events, taking part in workstreams, and contributing thought pieces to our magazine. Members can also join various work groups to collaborate and access networking opportunities that way.

What is the duration of the membership, and how do I renew it?

The duration is 1 year. You can set up auto renew so that you do not forget to renew the membership. You can discontinue auto renew any time and there is a cooling off period of 10 days.

When does my membership start?

Your membership starts the day it is approved. You will receive an email in your inbox stating that you have been accepted as a member. Your membership is valid for 1 year. If you are an Executive Council member, your membership commences from the day you receive your endorsement from the Executive Council.

How do I know if I am a startup?

You are considered a startup (workstream) member if your organisation is less than 5 years old and has fewer than 6 employees.

How many Workstreams can I join?

We have 13 Workstreams and you can join as many as you like. This can be done during the sign-up process, or later by updating your profile in our portal.
