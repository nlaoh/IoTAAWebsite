A data smart Australia

IoT Alliance Australia (IoTAA) is the peak industry body for IoT in Australia. We have a vision of a data smart Australia achieved by trusted, accessible, automated data through the use of IoT technologies, data and processes. We foster collaboration between IoT providers and users across industry, government, community and research. We promote good practice, raise awareness and advocate for responsible and innovative use of IoT.

Internet of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a transformative suite of technologies and data that, if appropriately and sensitively implemented, can help address the great social and ecological challenges of our time. The Internet of Things encompasses Industrial IoT, which is fundamental to Australia’s economy including critical infrastructure, manufacturing, cities and placemaking, construction, productivity and Consumer IoT. IoT creates, connects and processes data about devices, people, processes and things. It helps people make better and more informed decisions to advance Australia’s society, sustainability, productivity, competitiveness, jobs, and the economy.
